Project Management Tracker

Project Overview

Our management team requires a tool to effectively monitor project progress, focusing on budget and spending allocation, as well as mandays utilization. Presently, this information lacks transparency and is known only to a select few, potentially causing bottlenecks. Therefore, we have determined the necessity for a tool that provides a concise overview of all ongoing projects, enabling quick access to essential details.

Challenges

Data originates from diverse sources, each with its unique format, and ofter within the same category, multiple formats may exists. As a preliminary step, it's essential to standardize the structure of each data source and anticipate future utilization requirements. Flexibility in format is paramount to accommodate varying data sources efficiently.

Process

1. Scoping & Designing

The initial stage primarily involves engaging with other departments, understanding their accomplishments, and addressing their current challenges. It's imperative to accurately capture the needs of each department comprehensively. Additionally, I began exploring Asana, our project task tracker. My intention is to leverage Asana's data by utilizing its API to streamline and automate various processes.

2. Development

The developeed process entailed coding in Python, defining the workflow and its automation strategy, creating templates for data storages, and various other tasks.

3. Presentation

Presenting the system to other departments and capturing their feedback is crucial for future enhancements and improvements.

Result

The system is currently in use by various teams and serves as a reference for developing internal software to address areas not adequately covered by the existing system.

Data Stack

The project utilized a data stack consisting of Python, Asana, MySQL, and Google Sheet
