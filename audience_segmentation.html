Audience Segmentation Models

Project Overview

One challenge our client faces as their audience expands is identifying the appropriate target audience for their marketing campaigns. They regularly conduct newsletter campaigns, with some aimed at increasing brand awareness in the market. With their audience continually growing, they must pinpoint specific demographics to target with their newsletter.

Challenges

Initially, I purposed creating audience segmentation to gather insights into the characteristics of each group. This would enable us to determine which group best aligns with the newsletter's objectives. However, a challenge arose as the client lacked sufficient diverse data. They only possessed data on audience activities from past campaigns. To address this, I developed a clustering model using the K-Means algorithm for audience segmentation. Additionally, I created a Recency Frequency and Monetary (RFM) model as a comparison to the inital data model.

Process

1. Ideations

Identify the client issues, their expectation also the data they actually had.

2. Development

Developed clustering model using K-Means algorithm by utilizing audience activities data from past campaigns.

3. Evaluation & Selection

The K-Means model was actually used in the few next campaigns.

Result

Utilizing the first model-K-Means clustering model-we observed a threefold improvement in digital marketing campaign performance, marking a significant enhancement. However, for the second model employing the RFM algoritm, we have yet to conduct a real case study to test its effectiveness.
