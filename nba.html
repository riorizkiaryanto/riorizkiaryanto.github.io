Exploring Player Similarities: Clustering NBA Players

Project Overview

During my master's degree program, I undertook a project aimed at exploring and implementing machine learning techniques in real-world scenarios. Being passionate about basketball and intrigued by sport science, I choose a topic related to the NBA.

My project focused on utilizing machine learning, specifically clustering, to analyze NBA players. I segmented players into distinct groups based on similarities in their statistics. The inspiration behind this project stemmed from a statement made by an NBA coach suggesting that the traditional five positions in basketball could be condensed into two or three groups. This piqued my curiosity, driving me to investigate these proposed groupings and their relevance using clustering models.

Process

1. Data Preparation & Descriptive Analysis

The initial stage of any data project typically involves cleaning, standardizing, and selecting the data to be utilized in the data models. Additionally, conducting descriptive analysis aids in understanding the available data thoroughly.

2. Development

I wrote the code and developed the clustering model using Python, utilizing the K-Means algorithm, a commonly used clustering model.

3. Presentation

Present the findings.
